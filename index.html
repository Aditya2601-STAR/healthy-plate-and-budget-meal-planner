Why We Built This

Students often face a tough choice between eating healthy and staying within a tight budget. Most nutrition apps focus only on calorie tracking and completely ignore the financial side of things.

The Healthy Plate & Budget Meal Planner bridges this gap by combining nutritional guidance with real-time budget management — all in one simple web interface.

This project was built as part of our college mini project at Dilkap Research Institute of Engineering and Management Studies, affiliated with Suman Educational Trust.
